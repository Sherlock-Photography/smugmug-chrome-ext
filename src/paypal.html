Add PayPal buttons to this SmugMug gallery

Step one: Create your button on PayPal

On PayPal's website, log in to your account. Click on the "Merchant Services" tab, then click on the "Buy Now buttons" link on the right.

In Step 1, change the "button type" to "buy now" (if you only want to sell photos one at a time) or "shopping cart" (recommended).

Enter something for "item name", this will later be replaced by the URL of the photo your customer selected.

If you are offering multiple photo sizes or multiple product options, tick the "add drop-down menu with price" option and fill in the details you want displayed. Otherwise just enter the price for your single product.

Enter your shipping and tax information. Choose the option to use your secure merchant account ID.

In Step 2, untick the option to "save button at PayPal".

Now click "Create Button". On the next screen that appears, click the link "Remove code protection".

Finally, copy the code from the code box and paste it into the "Button HTML code" box below.

View cart button

If you are creating an "add to cart" button, you will also want to create a "view cart" button that your visitors can use to view the contents of their PayPal cart. Just after creating the "add to cart" button, you will see this banner at the bottom of the page:

Click on "create a view cart button". Choose the display options you like, then click "create button". Now copy the HTML code from the box that appears. You can paste that code into an HTML block in the SmugMug customiser to add the View Cart button to your website.
