Add PayPal buttons to this SmugMug gallery

Step one: Create a Buy Now button at PayPal

On PayPal's website, log in to your account. Click on the "Merchant Services" tab, then click on the "Buy Now buttons" link on the right.
In Step 1, change the "button type" to "Buy Now", and enter an Item Name.
If you are offering multiple photo sizes or multiple product options, tick the "add drop-down menu option" and fill in the menu details.
Enter shipping and tax information. Choose the option to use your secure merchant account ID (you can also choose the other option if you like, which would include your PayPal email address in your button code.)
In Step 2, untick the option to "save button at PayPal".
Now click "Create Button". On the next screen that appears, click the link "Remove code protection".
